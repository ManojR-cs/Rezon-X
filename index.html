About Our Club

Our club focuses on innovation, technical learning, teamwork, and project development. We organize workshops, hackathons, and participate in inter-college competitions. Our mission is to help students gain practical skills, leadership qualities, and industry exposure.

Activities & Events

Hackathons

From brainstorming bold ideas to building complete working prototypes, our team actively develops innovative solutions
during intense hackathon competitions, turning concepts into reality within hours.

Robo Soccer

We design and build robo soccer bots completely from scratch — integrating mechanical design, motor control, programming,
and wireless communication to compete in high-energy robotic soccer battles.

Line Follower Bot

Our members engineer autonomous line follower robots from the ground up, focusing on sensor integration,
circuit design, precision coding, and high-speed path tracking performance.

Aero Modelling

We create aerodynamic aircraft models from scratch by applying principles of flight,
structural design, balance, and propulsion to develop efficient and innovative flying systems.

Circuit building Workshop

Conducted technical workshops on Circuit building where students get hands on experiences by working with defferent kinds of Sensors.

CAD Workshop

Conducted technical workshops on CAD Designing where students gained hands-on
experience in creating 2D and 3D models using industry-standard design software and engineering design principles.

Projects

Crop care and KVK alert system

AI-Based Crop Disease Detection and KVK Alert System helps farmers detect crop diseases by uploading leaf images.
The YOLO model identifies the disease and suggests suitable remedies like pesticides or weedicides.
Using n8n, the detected disease and location are automatically sent to nearby Krishi Vigyan Kendra (KVKs) for monitoring and research purposes.

Multifunctional AgriBot

A compact prototype of a multifunctional agricultural robot designed for uneven Indian terrain.
The AgriBot integrates a pesticide spraying system with a camera to enable targeted and efficient application.
It supports crop surveillance, generates basic crop health insights, and can perform additional tasks such as grass cutting.
The project aims to evolve into a fully autonomous system with future integration of GPS-based navigation.

Voice controlled Bot

This project aims to promote student engagement in robotics through interactive voice-controlled bots.
The system enables basic voice interaction to answer queries and provide task reminders, offering a simple and accessible introduction to human–machine interaction and automation.

Setup for 3D filament with PET bottle

This project demonstrates a sustainable approach by converting used PET bottles into 3D printing filament.
It encourages students to think innovatively about waste utilization and environmental responsibility.
The setup produces low-cost filament, enabling greater accessibility to 3D printing while reducing material costs and plastic waste.

Smart Kiosk Printer

Develop a SmartPrint Kiosk System (Local Network Version) that allows users to upload documents, select print options, make UPI payments using a static QR code,
and receive automatic printouts via a USB-connected printer.
Additionally, the system includes an admin portal for monitoring jobs and a notification system for error alerts, ensuring reliability and real-time control.
